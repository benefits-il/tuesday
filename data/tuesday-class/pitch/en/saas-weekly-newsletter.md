Happy Monday, builders.

This week we are diving into the onboarding email sequence. If you are not sending these five emails, you are leaving activation on the table. Let's get into it.

1. THE WELCOME
Send it within 60 seconds of signup. Keep it human. One CTA, no menus.

2. THE QUICK WIN
Day 2. Show them one thing they can do in under five minutes that delivers value. Activation is everything.

3. THE SOCIAL PROOF
Day 4. Drop a customer story. People trust people, not features.

4. THE NUDGE
Day 7. For users who went quiet. Remind them what they are missing, gently.

5. THE UPGRADE
Day 14. Only after they have seen value. Ask for the sale once the habit is formed.
