# The pile map — for the instructor only (not part of the demo)

This is the raw INPUT pile for the presentations unit (3.4). The instructor feeds it into the Point agent to build an investor pitch deck for TUESDAY. The pile is messy on purpose: most files are signal, a few are noise. That filtering is the pedagogical point — the student sees that Point has to do real work to organize a real mess.

TUESDAY is a fictional teaching brand, work-management software for the one-person company. Not a real product.

## Signal — what Point should use

| File | What it is | Why it goes into the deck |
|---|---|---|
| `market-overview.md` | Freelance-market scan, rough TAM/SAM/SOM | Problem/market slide. The size of the opportunity. |
| `product-one-pager.md` | What TUESDAY does, the Tasks 2.0 angle, Customer Portal | Product/solution slide. |
| `traction-dump.md` | Narrative explanation of the numbers, messy | Traction slide, the context behind the chart. |
| `metrics.csv` | The monthly numbers table | Source for the charts (MRR, signups, churn). |
| `competitor-scan.md` | Monday / Notion / Trello vs TUESDAY | Competition and differentiation slide. |
| `founder-vision-note.md` | A personal, winding vision note, with tangents | Raw material for the story/why slide. Needs heavy cutting. |
| `the-ask.md` | How much we're raising and what for | The ask and use-of-funds slide. |
| `customer-quotes.md` | Customer quotes | Social proof, a slide or a strip inside a slide. |
| `pricing-snapshot.md` | Current plans and prices | Business model / pricing slide. |
| `brand-sheet.md` | Brand sheet (brought in from the tuesday brand pack) | Defines color, voice, logo. Affects the design, not slide content. |
| `press-boilerplate.md` | Official about text (brought in from brand-voice) | Dry company facts, founding year. Partly useful, inflated. |

## Noise — what Point should throw out

| File | What it is | Why it's noise |
|---|---|---|
| `support-ticket-thread.md` | An old support ticket about uploading a logo | Operational, not investor-related. |
| `saas-weekly-newsletter.md` | A generic marketing newsletter in English | Spam that landed in the folder, zero value for the deck. |
| `old-roadmap-q4.md` | A roadmap from two quarters ago, outdated | Misleading. Half of it was cancelled or changed. |

## What a good Point output looks like

A handoff of the deck structure in Markdown, not a designed deck. Inside it:

- Correct identification: ignore the three noise files, use the signal.
- An investor story arc: problem and market ← solution and product (Tasks 2.0 at the heart) ← traction with charts from metrics.csv ← competition and differentiation ← business model and pricing ← the ask and use of funds, with the personal story (cut down) as an emotional anchor near the start.
- Every slide with a title, a key point, and a source (which signal file).
- Keeping TUESDAY's dry voice, no buzzwords, no exclamation marks.
- Using the strong quotes (the airplane to the corner store, the pajamas) and not all of them.
- Treating the high churn as a risk being managed, not hiding it.

POWER then builds from the handoff. The target here is PowerPoint, and later Google Slides as well.
