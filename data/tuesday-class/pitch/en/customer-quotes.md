# Customer quotes (collected from emails and calls, with permission)

I kept here things customers said to me, most of them in answer to the question "what made you stay." I didn't edit, just fixed typos.

"I tried Monday and it was huge on me. I felt like I was paying for an airplane to fly to the corner store. With you I opened it and within ten minutes I was working. That's the difference."
— graphic designer, works alone

"The thing that got me is that the task is connected to the client and the invoice. Suddenly I see not just what to do, but for whom and what it's worth. It changed the way I think."
— business consultant

"I sent my client the portal and he said wow, you look like a pro. And I'm sitting at home in my pajamas. That alone was worth the subscription."
— event photographer

"I was in Notion for two years. I built myself a perfect system and never touched it. With you there's nothing to build, it's already built. For me that was everything."
— freelance developer

"I'm not an organized person. Tuesday didn't make me organized, but it catches me when I forget. It's the closest thing to an employee I've got."
— copywriter

Note to self: pick the two or three strongest for the deck. The "airplane to the corner store" quote and the "you look like a pro and I'm in pajamas" quote are the best — they tell the story without me having to explain.
