# Tuesday — brand sheet (rough)

Quick one so whatever we make looks like us. Not a full guideline doc, just the bones.

## Colors
- Yellow: #FDB913. This is the brand. Use it like you mean it.
- Black: somewhere around #0B0B0B. Backgrounds, type.
- White: for text on black.

That's the whole palette. No third color. If you're reaching for a third color, stop.

## Logo / mark
The Tiwaz rune — the up-arrow with a vertical stem (ᛏ). It's Tyr's mark, war and justice, the guy who handled it himself. We use it in yellow, geometric, clean. A small accent, not plastered over everything.

## Type
Heavy geometric sans, uppercase, tight. Bold and blocky. Nothing thin, nothing fancy, nothing handwritten.

## What we say
- Monday is for companies. Tuesday is for the one-person company.
- You don't have a manager. You are one.
- Start free. No card, no sales call.
- The salaried crowd starts on Monday. You've been fighting since Tuesday.

## Voice
Dry, direct, a little self-aware. We talk to the freelancer as a peer who knows the grind. No corporate gloss, no exclamation marks, no buzzwords.
