# Customer Survey — Quarterly Summary (Q2)

Sent to 4,000 active subscribers. 612 responded. Comparison against the previous quarter in parentheses.

## NPS
Overall score: 22 (previous quarter: 31).

A nine-point drop in a single quarter. This is not noise, it is a trend. The breakdown:
- Promoters: 38%
- Passives: 46%
- Detractors: 16% (was 9%)

The number of detractors has almost doubled.

## What people love most (open question, leading categories)
- "Simple and it works" — still the number one reason people stay
- The fair price
- The customer portal — many positive mentions, people are asking us to invest in it more
- The sense that the tool "gets freelancers"

## Leading complaints (open question)
- "Tasks 2.0 is heavy and confusing, I went back to the old view" — the number one complaint this year
- "You promised AI and it doesn't actually save me time"
- Sync bugs between devices
- No mobile app that feels good

## Most requested (open question)
1. Improve the customer portal — customers want invoices and payments there
2. A real mobile app
3. Less complexity, not more — "bring back the simplicity"
4. Calendar integration

## The bottom line from the survey
The people who love us love us because of the simplicity and because of the customer portal. "Tasks 2.0" is the single biggest source of complaints this year, not a source of love. The real demand is for other things.
