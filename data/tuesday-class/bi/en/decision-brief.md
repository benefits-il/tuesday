# TUESDAY — Decision Brief: Keep Building or Stop

From: Ron, VP Product
To: BI team
Date: June 14

I need an answer by Tuesday. We are at a crossroads.

## Background

Eight months ago we shipped "Tasks 2.0." We sold it hard, inside the company and out, as the product's step change. The team has now burned two quarters on it, and we have to decide whether it gets a third quarter or whether we stop and move those people somewhere else.

## What I need from you

Not opinions. Data. Pull together everything we have — usage, churn, surveys, competitors, money — and tell me what it says. No spin. If the numbers say stop, I want to know that now, not one more quarter from now.

## The single question to settle

Is it worth continuing to develop the product, and specifically to keep investing in "Tasks 2.0" — or not.

## Constraints to keep in mind

- Two developers are currently assigned to "Tasks 2.0." That is our most expensive team.
- Leadership wants to see a path to profitability by the end of the year.
- There is market pressure. The competitors are not sleeping.

Do not make the call yourselves. Bring me the full picture and I will decide with leadership. But bring it to me straight.
