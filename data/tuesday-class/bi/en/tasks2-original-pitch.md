# Tasks 2.0 — Original Announcement (for reference)

This is the marketing copy we launched "Tasks 2.0" with eight months ago. I put it in the folder so we remember what we promised, so we can compare it against what actually happened.

---

We are thrilled to announce the launch of Tasks 2.0, a groundbreaking module purpose-built to empower our users to seamlessly streamline their workflows and unlock unprecedented levels of productivity.

Leveraging cutting-edge AI-powered technology, Tasks 2.0 delivers a best-in-class, end-to-end experience designed to help the modern freelancer maximize efficiency and scale their operations like never before.

Key benefits include enhanced functionality, robust integrations, and an intuitive user experience that drives measurable results.

---

## Why I kept this
Notice the gap. The promise was "simplicity" and "time savings." In the survey, the number one complaint is that "Tasks 2.0" is heavy and confusing, and that the AI doesn't really save time. The gap between the promise and what customers actually feel is part of the story here.
