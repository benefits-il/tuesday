Tuesday — who the people are

The team is still small enough that you can meet everyone in a week. Here are the short lines, the rest you'll pick up by the coffee.

Dana, People and Ops. The first person you'll meet and the one who remembers every name. If something isn't working, access, laptop, anything, Dana is the address. Also the one who makes the good coffee.

Yoav, founder. Built Tuesday because he was tired of running his freelance work through three apps and a sticky note. Still writes code sometimes, mostly when told not to. Direct, fast, and hates long meetings exactly as much as you do.

Maya, Product. Decides what we build and in what order, and why we're not building the brilliant idea that came up yesterday. If you have a question about why the product works one way and not another, she'll be glad you asked.

Itay, Engineering. Turns Maya's decisions into things that actually work. He's the one who built Tasks 2.0. If you're on the technical team, you'll be spending a lot of time with him.

Roni, Marketing and Community. Writes everything that goes out in the dry, direct voice you're starting to recognize. If you're wondering who wrote the welcome email you got when you signed up for the product, that's her.

Noa, Customer Success. Talks to the freelancers who actually use us, and brings their pain back inside. The best-informed person in the company, she knows what people really do with the product.
