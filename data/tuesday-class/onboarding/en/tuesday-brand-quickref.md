Tuesday — brand quick reference (so the deck looks like us)

Not a full brand guide, just the bones, so anything we produce looks like Tuesday.

Colors
- Yellow #FDB913. This is the brand. Use it like you mean it.
- Black, roughly #0B0B0B. Backgrounds, text.
- White, for text on black.
That's the whole palette. There's no third color. If you reach for a third color, stop.

The symbol
The Tiwaz rune, the arrow pointing up with the vertical stem. The mark of Tyr, the god who handled war and the doing of justice entirely on his own. In yellow, geometric, clean. A small accent, not smeared over everything.

Typography
Heavy geometric sans, large letters, tight. Bold and blocky. Nothing thin, nothing ornamented, nothing handwritten.

What we say
- Monday is for companies. Tuesday is for the one-person company.
- You don't have a manager. You are one.
- Start free. No card, no sales call.

The voice
Dry, direct, a little self-aware. We talk like a peer who knows the grind. No corporate gloss, no exclamation marks, no buzzwords.
