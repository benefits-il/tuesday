Tuesday — first-week checklist

You don't have to finish all of this on day one. This is a list for the week. Go through it at your own pace, check off what you've done, and tell Dana if anything is stuck.

First day at work
- Show up, find the place, meet Dana for coffee
- Get your laptop and your first set of access
- Log into Slack and introduce yourself in the general channel, one sentence, no more
- Look at the values doc and at the product itself, no pressure to understand everything

By mid-week
- Finish setting up all the accounts from the tools and access list
- Sit down with the buddy assigned to you for a first conversation
- Walk through the first sample customer inside Tuesday, just to get a feel for the product from the inside
- Meet at least two people from other teams, even if only for five minutes

By the end of the week
- Your first real task, small and well-defined, your buddy will hand it to you
- Submit it and get feedback, because that's how people learn here
- Write Dana three lines, what was good about the week and what was confusing, we get better from that

That's it. The first week isn't measured by how much you got done. It's measured by reaching the end of the week feeling like you know where things are and who to ask.
