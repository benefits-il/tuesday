Tuesday — tools and access

What you get, and where everything lives. If something on this list isn't working by the middle of your first week, write Dana and she'll chase it down.

What's waiting for you, ready
- A laptop, set up in advance, with what you need already installed
- An email address on the company domain
- A Slack account, this is where most of the day-to-day communication happens
- Access to an internal Tuesday account, so you get to know our product from the inside as a user
- An office entry card

Where things live
- All daily back-and-forth, in Slack. A general channel for everyone, channels by team, and direct messages for whatever doesn't need an audience
- Documents, decks, and shared files, on the company shared drive, in folders by team
- Tasks and projects, inside Tuesday itself, we manage ourselves in our own product
- Passwords, in the company password manager, Dana will add you to it. No password spreadsheets, please

A few simple rules
- Don't keep work files only on your local machine, everything goes to the shared drive
- If you opened an account in an external tool in the company's name, tell Dana so it gets documented
- When you're not sure which Slack channel something belongs in, the general channel is always a safe answer
