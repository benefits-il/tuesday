Tuesday — note from the founder (Yoav wrote this one morning, we left it untouched)

Hi,

You signed up to work here, which means one of two things happened. Either you saw what we're building and believed in it, or someone convinced you it's a good place and you took their word for it. Either way, welcome.

Here's the thing. I built Tuesday because I broke. I was running my freelance work through three different apps and a notes file I called "urgent urgent urgent," and one day I'd just had enough. The big companies start their week on Monday, with managers and committees and stand-up meetings. The freelancer is already fighting by Tuesday, alone. We built the product for them.

What I want you to take from this note is this. We treat you the way we treat our customers. No hovering, no fourteen-step setup wizard, and no "let's hop on a quick demo call" when you could just say the thing. You'll get real responsibility fast, you'll make mistakes, and you'll learn from them. That's how it works here.

The product won't do the work for our customers, we checked. And I won't do yours for you either. But I will make sure you have everything you need to do it well.

Go get them.

Yoav
