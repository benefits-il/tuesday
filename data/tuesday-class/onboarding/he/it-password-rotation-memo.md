INTERNAL IT NOTICE — Quarterly Password Rotation

To: All staff
From: IT (outsourced, via the shared mailbox)
Re: Scheduled credential rotation — Q3

This is the routine quarterly reminder. Per the security policy, all staff passwords for the shared drive and internal admin tools expire at the end of this month and must be reset.

What you need to do:
- Reset your shared-drive password before the 30th. After that date the old one stops working and you will be locked out until you reset it.
- Use the company password manager to store the new one. Do not reuse a previous password. Do not keep passwords in a spreadsheet or a sticky note.
- Minimum 14 characters, at least one number and one symbol.
- Two-factor stays on. If your authenticator app was reinstalled, re-enroll before the deadline.

If you are locked out after the deadline, open a ticket in the shared IT mailbox and someone will get to it within one business day. Do not message individual team members for resets; they cannot do it.

This memo is sent every quarter. If you already rotated this cycle, ignore it.
