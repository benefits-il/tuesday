# /carousel (5-slide branded Instagram carousel)

Paste this into Cowork with the Tuesday plugin installed and the brand pack attached. The visual is described down to the pixel on purpose. A free-tier image model only delivers when you leave it nothing to guess, so every slide spells out the exact background, color, type, mark, and text placement.

---

```
/carousel

Build a 5-slide Instagram carousel for Tuesday, the work-management app for solo people running the whole show. All five slides must look like one set: identical system, only the text changes.

Read these attached files first:
- brand-sheet.md: Tuesday's colors, mark, type, and voice.
- carousel-outline.md: the exact headline and sub-line for each of the five slides.

Generate FIVE separate square images, 1:1, 1080x1080, in order, slide 1 through slide 5.

EXACT VISUAL SYSTEM, applied identically to every slide:
- Background: flat near-black, hex #0B0B0B, edge to edge. No gradient, no texture, no noise, no photograph, no drop shadow.
- Only two colors appear anywhere on the canvas: near-black #0B0B0B and bright Tuesday yellow #FDB913. No white, no third color, no other accent.
- Headline: heavy geometric sans-serif, all uppercase, black/heavy weight, tight letter-spacing, in yellow #FDB913. Placed in the upper-center, large, filling most of the width with a comfortable margin. This is the loudest thing on the slide.
- Sub-line: same geometric sans, uppercase, much smaller (about one quarter the headline size), in yellow #FDB913, placed directly under the headline with clear spacing. Never larger than the headline.
- The Tiwaz mark: a single vertical stem with an upward arrowhead at the top (the rune ᛏ, read as an up-arrow), drawn geometric and clean in yellow #FDB913, small, sitting in the bottom-right corner of every slide in the exact same position and size. It is an accent, never large, never centered.
- Generous even margins on all four sides. Text centered horizontally. Nothing touches the edges.

EXACT TEXT PER SLIDE (use these strings verbatim from carousel-outline.md; headline on top, sub-line below):
- Slide 1: headline: "YOU DON'T HAVE A MANAGER." sub: "You are one. And the accountant. And the guy who fixes the printer."
- Slide 2: headline: "SO EVERYTHING LIVES EVERYWHERE." sub: "Clients in your inbox. Invoices in a spreadsheet. The actual plan in your head, mostly."
- Slide 3: headline: "ONE PLACE FOR CLIENTS." sub: "Who they are, what is open, what they owe. No more digging through email."
- Slide 4: headline: "ONE PLACE FOR MONEY." sub: "Invoices sent, invoices paid, invoices pretending they didn't see it. Tuesday keeps score."
- Slide 5: headline: "START TUESDAY." sub: "Free. No card, no demo call. Just the button."

Consistency check before you finish: same background hex, same yellow hex, same font, same headline size, same sub-line size, same Tiwaz mark in the same corner on all five. If any slide drifts, regenerate that slide only.

Show me all five slides in order so I can confirm they read as one set before I publish.
```
