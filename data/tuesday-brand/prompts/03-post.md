# /post (one Instagram post: image plus caption)

Paste this into Cowork with the Tuesday plugin installed and the brand pack attached. The visual-producer builds the image, the content-writer writes the caption, and they come back as one finished post.

---

```
/post

Build one finished Instagram post for Tuesday, the work-management app for solo people running the whole show. I want both the image and the caption, on brand and ready to publish.

Read these attached files before starting:
- brand-voice.md: the Tuesday voice guidelines from station 1. This is the law for the caption's voice.
- brand-sheet.md: Tuesday's colors, mark, and type. This is the law for the image.
- social-seed.md: this week's topic and the sample post lines. Use it as the starting point, do not copy a sample line word for word.

VISUAL (visual-producer). Generate one square 1:1 image:
- Background: near-black, flat, around #0B0B0B. No gradient, no texture, no photo.
- One short line of text, centered, in heavy geometric uppercase sans, bright Tuesday yellow #FDB913. Tight letter-spacing, bold and blocky. The line comes from the caption's hook, kept very short (six words or fewer).
- The Tiwaz mark (ᛏ, a vertical stem with an up-arrow head, like an upward arrow) small in yellow in the bottom-right corner. Clean, geometric, not plastered large.
- Only two colors on the canvas: near-black and yellow. No third color. Generous margins, nothing crowding the edges.

CAPTION (content-writer):
- Tuesday's voice from brand-voice.md: dry, true, peer to peer. Open from the side, one parenthetical aside that lands, no buzzwords, no exclamation marks, no emojis, no em-dashes.
- Three to four short lines, ending on a line that stamps it. The hook line should match the text on the image.
- One quiet sign-off pointing to Tuesday (start free, no card), no hashtag soup. At most one or two plain hashtags if any.

Show me the image and the caption together so I can check they read as one post before I publish anything.
```
