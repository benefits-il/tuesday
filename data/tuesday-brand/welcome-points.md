# Welcome email points (input for /welcome)

Core bullets for the automated welcome email. This is not the finished message.

## Who gets this
A user who just created a Tuesday account. They made the decision, which is the hard part. Now they are looking at an empty workspace, wondering if they made a mistake.

## What we welcome them for
Moving their business out of the three-apps-and-a-notes-file setup. That is the win. Acknowledge it without making a scene.

## The first step
Add your first client. That is all. Just one. No project plans, no product tours, no fourteen-step onboarding wizards. There is a button on the screen. They will find it.

## What NOT to promise
Tuesday does not do the work. It will not 10x their business. It keeps clients, projects, and invoices in one place so they stop losing track. Focus on utility, not transformation.

## Tone cue
Warm. Slightly warmer than the outreach, since they already signed up. Still dry, still a peer. A welcome note they might actually read instead of archive. No "thrilled," no "exciting journey."

## Sign-off
From a real person, not a "team of experts." Match the spirit of the existing welcome mail in `raw/welcome-email.md`.
