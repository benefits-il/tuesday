# Social seed (input for /post and the weekly set)

A theme and a few sample lines. This is the seed, not the finished copy.

## This week's topic
The invoice that sits there unpaid. Getting paid as a freelancer. The quiet dread of the follow-up note. The client who wants to pay in exposure. The stuff solo operators feel but rarely post about.

## Why it fits Tuesday
Tuesday tracks who owes you what. It nudges the follow-up so you do not have to write the "just circling back" email for the third time. We cannot fix the client. We just make the chase less lonely.

## Sample post lines (Tuesday voice, starting points only)
- "Sent the invoice. Checked the bank app. Checked it again. We built Tuesday so you only have to do the first part."
- "Freelancing means being your own worst boss. The one who forgets to pay you on time. Tuesday handles the reminders."
- "Net 30 is a beautiful concept. So is the tooth fairy. Tuesday tracks which clients believe in the same things you do."

## Tone cue
Dry, honest, and a little tired. No hashtag soup. Every line must earn its place. One yellow Tiwaz mark on the visual is enough.
