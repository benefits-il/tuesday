# Outreach context (input for /outreach)

Rough notes for a cold email. This is not the email itself, just the core message.

## Who we are writing to
Solo operators running the show. Freelance designers, consultants, one-person studios. People whose business lives in three different apps and a desktop note titled "URGENT." They probably found us through a newsletter or a friend. They have not signed up yet. They are busy, skeptical, and have ignored forty productivity pitches this week.

## The ask (left open for the plugin to sharpen)
Get them to try Tuesday. No hard pitch. No overselling. Just a direct, peer-to-peer note with a single reason to click.

## The key message
Tuesday keeps your clients, projects, and invoices in one place. It will not do the work for you. Nothing does. We checked. But it keeps you from losing track of who owes you money and what is due on Thursday. Start free. No credit card, no demo calls.

## Tone cue
Peer to peer. We know the grind because we live it. Dry, warm, zero hype. The absolute opposite of a corporate announcement.

`raw/feature-announcement.md` (the corporate "Tasks 2.0" template). If outreach mentions a feature, pull the facts from there. Do not copy the tone. That tone is the problem.
